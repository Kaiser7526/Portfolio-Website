About Me

Hi, I'm Kaiser Zahoor. I am a passionate software developer with a background in computer science and a strong interest in web development and technology. I graduated from Vemana Institute of Technology, Bangalore with a degree in Computer Science and Engineering, where I gained solid fundamentals in algorithms, data structures, and software engineering principles.

During my academic journey, I actively participated in coding competitions and projects that honed my problem-solving skills and exposed me to various programming languages and frameworks. I specialize in front-end development, proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React.js.

Outside of coding, I enjoy exploring new technologies, attending tech meetups, and contributing to open-source projects. I am enthusiastic about creating user-friendly interfaces and delivering high-quality software solutions.
